About Viscrow Health

Transforming Healthcare Documentation Through AI Innovation

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare documentation by leveraging cutting-edge AI technology, enabling healthcare providers to focus more on patient care and less on administrative tasks.

Our Vision

To become the global standard in AI-powered medical documentation, reducing physician burnout and improving healthcare delivery worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a team of healthcare professionals and AI experts, Viscrow Health emerged from firsthand experience with the challenges of medical documentation. We witnessed how administrative burdens were affecting patient care quality and physician well-being.

Today, we're proud to offer a solution that combines state-of-the-art AI technology with deep medical expertise, serving healthcare providers across various specialties.
